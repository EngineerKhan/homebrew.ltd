# Team


At Homebrew, we are thinkers and tinkerers who work on building human-augmented AI.

We're a small, fully-remote team, mostly based in the GMT+8 Timezone (Asia). 

We are committed to become a global company. Check our [Careers page](https://homebrew.bamboohr.com/careers) if you'd like to join us on our adventure.
